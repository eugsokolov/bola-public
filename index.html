Bola Buddy is a smart mooring system designed for modern boating. It allows boaters to reserve, pay for, and securely lock their vessels to a mooring buoy using a mobile or web app. Once you reserve a mooring spot, the buoy is exclusively assigned to you, and nobody else can unlock it, ensuring that your mooring ball is available when you arrive. The system simplifies the mooring process and offers premium add-on services for an enhanced boating experience.

The smart locking mechanism is integrated into the mooring buoy and can be controlled through the Bola Buddy app. Once the boater arrives at their pre-reserved mooring spot, the buoy communicates with the boaterâ€™s device, and the locking mechanism is activated, securely fastening the boat to the mooring point. The locking system prevents unauthorized users from accessing the buoy.

Bola Buddy eliminates the complexities of traditional mooring systems. Its deck-level design allows you to secure the buoy with one hand, without the need for reaching or bending.
